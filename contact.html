What to include in your message

The more detail you provide, the faster we can help. For bug reports: your browser name and version, your operating system, what you were doing when the issue occurred, and whether it happens every time or occasionally.

For feature requests: describe the problem you're trying to solve, not just the solution. "I want a metronome" is good; "I want a way to practice typing at a consistent rhythm because I tend to burst-and-pause" is better — it helps us find the best solution.

For general feedback: anything goes. We love hearing how people use TyperTester and what they think could be improved.
