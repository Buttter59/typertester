Our Story

Built by typists,
for typists.

TyperTester exists because the best typing tests we found were full of ads, required accounts, used random word soup, or gave you no useful data after the test.

Mission

Why we built this.

Typing is the primary physical interface between human thought and digital output. Virtually every knowledge worker, student, writer, developer, and creator types for a large portion of their day. Yet most people never deliberately practice it — and never know how slow they actually are.

TyperTester is different. We use real passages — actual stories, science, history, and philosophy — so practice feels engaging rather than mechanical. We show you a speed graph so you can see your momentum. We compare your score to global benchmarks. We teach you proper finger placement with our hand guide. And we give you a clear path forward through four difficulty levels.

No account needed. No ads. Free, forever. Questions or feedback: typertesterweb@gmail.com
